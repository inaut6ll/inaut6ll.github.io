Recipe #1: Cheesy Broccoli

  • Time: However long you deem necessary
  • Serves: A nation full of dogs
Ingredients Quantity
Cheese 300 liters
Broccoli 4000000
Faith (*very important) 60 ounces

Step 1: Put all the cheese in a pot.
Step 2: Put all the broccoli in the same pot.
Step 3: Set the pot on the stove and let it sit there.

Step 5: When it's done, pour the contents of the pot onto the ground.
